There are four focuses to Orbis Labs:

Both virtually and at each of our workstations (Hackerspaces), teams are grouped into one of four focuses.

Software, mobile apps, and artificial intelligence research fall under the duty of the software engineers. Web developers build and maintain sites for customers and in house projects. Information security covers both red team and blue team in defending and pen testing in house projects, as well as customers whom request the service. Engineers partner with our sister company Vulcan Robotics to build and maintain telecommunications systems, robotics, devices, and other forms of hardware.
